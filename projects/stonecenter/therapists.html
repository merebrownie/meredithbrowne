Leslie is the Director of The Stone Center for Counseling & Leadership and a Clinical Supervisor. She has worked as a Licensed Professional Counselor since 1996. Her clinical experience includes working with children, individuals, couples and families using a systemic and internal family systems approach. Her "4 Kids Sake Parenting Programs" were developed to assist parents with the various challenges they face in dealing with each developmental stage and to facilitate cooperative co-parenting when families are going through a divorce or separation. Prior to moving to Charlotte in 2001, Leslie maintained a private practice in Austin, Texas. Her specialized training as a play therapist and marriage and family therapist serve her well in her focus with children, couples and individuals who are dealing with grief and loss, depression, anxiety, trauma, adjustment and relational issues. Additionally, she works with special needs children and their families, children and families experiencing divorce, and children with behavioral issues in school, for which she provides parenting consultation, coaching and school observation/remediation services. Leslie conducts parenting workshops, marriage seminars and presentations related to these and other issues.

Leslie is also a Board Certified Coach and offers life coaching services. She specializes in coaching working mothers who seek to find their passion, live more fully, decrease their stress and overwhelm and increase their life fulfillment. Leslie is available to conduct workshops and seminars to your group, company or organization related to increasing productivity, teamwork, leadership skills, and managing work and life (she believes work/life balance is a myth).

Contact Leslie at: leslie@thestonecenternc.com
Andrea H. Miller is a Licensed Professional Counselor, Registered Play Therapist, and Co-Director at The Stone Center for Counseling & Leadership. Prior to joining The Stone Center, Andrea worked in an agency setting providing services to children, adolescents, and families in the community and in the schools. She has experience working with grief issues, behavioral issues, attachment issues, trauma, depression and anxiety.

She uses a supportive, holistic approach when working with clients. Andrea believes the most important part of the counseling process is the counselor-client relationship. She believes healing occurs through a respectful, collaborative approach that allows for self-exploration. Andrea's approach comes from a relational model of counseling, while also using techniques that meet the needs of each individual client. She hopes to help clients discover and learn to trust their own wisdom through the counseling process.

In her work with children, Andrea primarily uses play therapy. She views the healing process for children to include a variety of different factors: social, emotional, psychological, and other environmental factors. She is passionate about helping families through a supportive, collaborative process that promotes growth and awareness in the child. She also believes strengthening the child-parent relationship is essential for healing and growth.

Contact Andrea at: andrea@thestonecenternc.com
Katie is a Licensed Professional Counselor Associate. Before she began working at The Stone Center, Katie worked as a Behavioral Therapist with individuals who have special needs. In 2010, she began working towards her Masters in Clinical Counseling at Columbia International University. During her time there, she concentrated her studies on child and adolescent therapy with a special focus on sexual abuse. Katie has also worked as an Applied Behavioral Therapist with children with Autism for two years. During her time at The Stone Center, Katie has had the opportunity to work with individuals, children, couples, and families dealing with depression, anxiety, marital discord, anger, trauma, ADHD, and various life transitions. She believes in a holistic approach that focuses on integrating the biological, social, psychological, emotional, and spiritual factors in an effort to facilitate growth and healing in her clients.

Contact Katie at: katie@thestonecenternc.com
